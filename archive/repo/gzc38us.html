RangerHikes Posted at 0:19 UTC on May 25, 2021 (Permalink)

No. Body building is good for getting bigger and nothing else. Dense muscle is inflexible and heavy mass is bad for moving fast. You'll notice athletes have an easier time going from sports to body building than the other way around. Also consider that body building is about astetics, where as competitive weight lifting / strongman is about being as strong as possible. And you'll notice while those guys are terrifyingly powerful, they don't tend to be successful in other sports.

If you wanna be a sprinter, train to be a sprinter. Vanity muscle is worthless outside of just about everything except contests to see who has the prettiest vanity muscle
