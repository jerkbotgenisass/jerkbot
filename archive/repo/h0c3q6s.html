I'm new to keto but I have been into lifting for bodybuilding and powerlifting for 25 years.

I started my weight lifting journey with the 3 sets of 12 to 15 reps. For about a year at age 19 I could put on muscle and strength for sure.

As you get conditioned your target range becomes a few less reps for sure. So 8 to 10 reps should be a target.

Some people prefer 4 sets on an exercise. Based on genetics of body type and muscle fiber type you have creatine/bun ratio that blood work shows. The mix of mesomorph, Endo, ecto etc. Fast and slow twitch fibers.

I have done 5x5. 10x10. 3 rep powerlifting. But for me to feel and look the best I superset with back to back training e.g. chest and back.. and I pyramid (increase weight on each set). And I like to reverse pyramid ( decrease weight on each set ) to get the pump and blood flow etc

5x5 is for power. Do it for a month and write down track your progress. Shock your body with higher reps, different types of frequency, reps, weight, sets, tempo, speed of concentric and eccentric movements.
