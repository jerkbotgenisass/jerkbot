pooley12 Posted at 13:14 UTC on June 9, 2021 (Permalink)

I see very little point in doing sumo whatsoever if your goal is athletic performance. It puts you through a lesser range of motion, in an extremely unnatural position.

The trap bar deadlift makes more sense for athletes. I don’t understand the carryover from sumo to athletic performance ? I don’t think sumo has a place in an athletes program unless they physically cannot do a conventional deadlift. Even still, if you’re trying to achieve improved speed and power for athleticism in general, neither deadlift is probably the best exercise for you.
