Unpopular opinion but the obsession with the wiki programs on this sub is nauseating. Don't get me wrong, they're all proven and get results. But for someone who's just starting out, they might see it and get overwhelmed or not have the time to do it and track progress.

I always see beginners asking questions here and then every reply is "Read the wiki, follow program, end of discussion". It just ends any potential for having a dialogue about fitness. I think the better advice is to start off doing exercises that you enjoy and move onto a proper program later. Working out should be fun and starting a strict program from day 1 just sucks the fun out of it and most people are very unlikely to actually stick to it (in my opinion).
