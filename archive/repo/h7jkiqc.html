Editing to explain a bit more

Unless you’re powerlifting in a competition and need that extra boost for the winning prize money… NEVER arch please. Arching is the worse thing you can do for your body. It exposes multiple failure points across the body for injuries.

“Arching on the bench is a bridge to the hospital.” That quote only means something if you arch improperly. Just don’t over arch and you’ll be okay. There is a natural arch in the human body when benching.
