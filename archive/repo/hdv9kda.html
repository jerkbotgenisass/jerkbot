Squats are fun and great, a lot times just not worth the risk. With age exercise selection is a must for longevity, if you want to lift for health I would say cycle them off every 6 weeks. Use jump squats, alternating jump lunge, one leg lunge , leg extensions and leg curls and you will get the alot of leg benefits and not be feeling beat up. Once you have some stronger legs go back to squats, is the risk worth the reward?
