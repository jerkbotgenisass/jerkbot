Few_Echidna_1271 Posted at 20:28 UTC on September 29, 2021 (Permalink)

“Big meals” is too broad a term.. every person is different. Find your maintenance cals first. Then add 300-500 per day .. timing of meals doesn’t really matter as much as people make it out to.. just wanna make sure you’re getting some protein within an hour or so of your workout (before or after) and eat some post-workout carbs to replenish glycogen

Oh and eat roughly 1g of protein per 1lb of body weight a day.. could go a little more or a little less, but that’s a solid baseline
